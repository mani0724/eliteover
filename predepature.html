Pre departure service

Upon securing a student visa, the pre-departure phase emerges as a pivotal step, especially for first-time travelers. Elite Overseas acknowledges the significance of this transition and is dedicated to ensuring both students and their parents are well-prepared for the journey ahead. Through meticulously organized pre-departure seminars and sessions, we address concerns regarding accommodation, post-landing procedures, and university orientation. Our aim is to alleviate any doubts or fears, fostering confidence and peace of mind as students embark on their educational endeavors abroad.

Elite Overseas organise pre departure seminar and sessions to help parents and student to clear all doubts and fear so they can travel smoothly too their preferred destination and university. Often we understand student travelling on student visa are nervous, therefore we also ask our previous students or universities to greet new students on the airport.

Value Added Services

At Elite Overseas, our array of value-added services extends beyond facilitating your journey to study abroad. We offer comprehensive assistance with Educational Loans, Financial Aid, Pre-Departure Briefings, Forex, Travel, and Accommodation, ensuring a seamless transition to your desired destination. Our commitment doesn't end upon arrival; we prioritize your comfort and confidence, providing support to help you settle in smoothly. Through our extensive alumni network, you can gain valuable insights into the lifestyle and culture of your study abroad destination, enabling you to adapt to the changes with ease. With Elite Overseas, you're not just embarking on an academic journey—you're gaining a supportive network dedicated to your success every step of the way.
